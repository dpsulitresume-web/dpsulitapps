About the Study

Struggling with type 2 diabetes? Join this research study to explore a new potential treatment that aims to lower blood sugar and body weight. With type 2 diabetes significantly increasing the risk of cardiovascular disease, finding effective treatments is crucial.

Participants will stay close to potentially transforming diabetes care while receiving close health monitoring and support.

Who Can Participate?

  • People aged 18-75 diagnosed with type 2 diabetes
  • Must have been on a stable daily dose of Metformin
  • Must have HbA1c of 7.5% - 10%
  • Must not have been diagnosed with diabetic retinopathy or maculopathy
